TTRPGS

What are ttrpgs? Click here to find out!

Why do I like TTRPGs?

TTRPGs bring me and my friends to the table and tie us together through roleplay as we embody characters akin to actors embodying roles and deal with situations we wouldn't ordinarily be able to deal with.

My Favorite TTRPGs, Ranked

Click on the links to go to the page

  1. Blades in the Dark

    A game by John Harper about a crew of daring scoundrels seeking their fortunes on the haunted streets of an industrial-fantasy city.

  2. Pathfinder 2

    A sword and sorcery game made by Paizo filled with adventure in a high fantasy world.

  3. Lancer

    Made by Abbadon and Massif Press, this game puts you in the seat of a mecha for tactical combat fun

  4. FFG Star Wars

    A game by FFG in the widely known Star Wars universe, taking the role of scoundrels, rebels or Jedi taking on the empire

  5. Stewpot

    A calming rules light ttrpg by Takuma Okada where you play retired adventurers running a fantasy tavern

  6. Thirsty Sword Lesbians

    Brought to us by April Kit Walsh, this is a game about Thirsty Lesbians clashing blades
